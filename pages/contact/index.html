And that is simply it: 9 times out of 10, the iris wins. It’s the choice of champions. We stare at it, enjoy it, get enveloped by it, and the soul that seems to peek out at us from its depths, we greet it with a warm smile and murmur to ourselves “fuck an email, this is it, this is what life is all about.
